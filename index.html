John's Amazing Quesadilla Recipe

John's Amazing Quesadilla

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of shredded of cheese
  • 1 pack of tortillas
  • 1 stick of butter
  • an apron (not for consumption)
  • 8 oz of chicken

Description

This is the best quesadilla you will ever have. It is cheap to make and tastes amazing. Here is the link to the Wikipedia page for my world famous quesadilla.

Directions

  1. Put on your apron (you're going to need it)
  2. Put a pan on the stove and set the heat to medium.
  3. Place 1/8 of a stick of butter on the pan.
  4. Once the butter has melted, place a tortilla on the pan.
  5. Spread cheese evenly on one half of the tortilla.
  6. Place pieces of chicken on the tortilla (hopefully your chicken is cooked)
  7. Fold the tortilla in half and cover one side with the other.
  8. Wait until a side is golden brown and then flip.
  9. Take the quesadilla off the pan, wipe your hands on the apron, and enjoy!
